Use of the app

Furniture Planner helps create furniture concepts, layouts, estimates and documents. You are responsible for verifying all dimensions, materials, clearances, prices and production data before manufacturing or ordering.

Production verification

Exports, cut lists, estimates and visualizations are planning aids. Before production, confirm measurements, hardware requirements, installation conditions and material availability with a qualified professional.
